\subsection{Detailed Description}
Displays dialogue lines to the player, and sends user choices back to the dialogue system. 

Note that this is just one way of presenting the dialogue to the user. The only hard requirement is that you provide the Run\-Line, \hyperlink{a00147}{Run\-Options}, Run\-Command and Dialogue\-Complete coroutines; what they do is up to you.
